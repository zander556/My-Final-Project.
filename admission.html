_______________For College Admission Procedures______________

Freshmen Go online to zgis@gmail.com Click the “New Student – College“ Carefully fill out the Application Form with complete and truthful information. Go to your email inbox to view the Acknowledgment email. Click the link/button to verify your email address. Take note of your Application Reference Number, the subjects and approximate assessment. Use the link to update your profile; and optionally, upload digital copies of documents. Download, print, and complete the Certificate of Financial Responsibility and Personal Statement Form
Continuing Students / Old Studen Go to zgis@gmail.com and follow the relative button/link for Continuing Students
Transferees Follow the Application Process for Freshmen Send an email to zgis@gmail.com with the following: Subject: Transferee for Evaluation Include in the message: Full Name and Reference Number(Reference Number provided in the application) Attachment: Clear Scanned Copy of OTR or Certificate of Grades You will receive an acknowledgment from your Admissions Advisor.
